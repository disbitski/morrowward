# Under-three-minute demo script

Target runtime: **2:40–2:50**. Never exceed 2:59.

## 0:00–0:22 — Why

“I grew up without much and was diagnosed with Type 1 diabetes at ten, so planning for the future became real very early. That same year, paper-route savings bought this Commodore 64. Small daily experiments in BASIC changed my life. Morrowward applies that lesson to financial literacy: small steps can change what your future looks like.”

Show the mission photo briefly, then move immediately into the product.

## 0:22–0:58 — Horizon Reveal

“Morrowward is a local-first financial future simulator. There is no account and no real money. I choose my experience level, current and target ages, starting balance, and a weekly habit.”

Complete onboarding and reveal the three illustrative futures. Point out contributions, growth, inflation-adjusted value, and the non-advice label.

## 0:58–1:30 — Weekly practice

“The point is not to predict a stock price. It is to make a repeatable habit visible. I add this week’s simulated contribution and practice a fractional purchase.”

Deposit the weekly amount, buy one demo asset, and show simulated cash/holdings and updated progress.

## 1:30–1:58 — Financial literacy with GPT-5.6

Ask: “Why does starting earlier matter more than finding the perfect investment?”

“GPT-5.6 explains the concept at the experience level I chose. The response is schema-constrained, states assumptions, and cannot place trades or give individualized buy/sell instructions. Without an API key, the app still works and supplies deterministic educational fallbacks.”

Show the daily brief’s separate facts, sentiment, uncertainty, and takeaway.

## 1:58–2:30 — Codex and implementation quality

“Codex helped turn the idea into a working product: it audited patterns from our earlier projects, designed the local-first architecture, implemented the deterministic finance engine, built the responsive PWA, created property tests and AI safety evaluations, and documented every major decision. GPT-5.6 educates; tested code owns the math.”

Flash the test output, architecture section in the README, and build journal.

## 2:30–2:45 — Close

“Morrowward is not a promise about markets. It is a way to see that a small action today can still matter decades from now. Small steps. A future you can see.”

End on the Horizon Reveal and repository/website URLs.
